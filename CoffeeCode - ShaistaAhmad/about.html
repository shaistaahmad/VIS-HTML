About our Team

A little bit about our humble beginnings, and about our individual members.

We're a sincere company with a straightforward vision. We believe that there is an amazing amount to be learned by working in a "real world" web production environment, alongside other like-minded teams. With this in mid, we began to gather people together in October of 2008. From there, the group quickly expanded to nine members, and we decided upon the name "Coffee n' Code". After a slow start, we found many solid, dedicated members who are in the group today.
